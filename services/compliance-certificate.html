ROHS CERTIFICATION

Introduction

ROHS refers for the Restriction of Hazards Substances. It is designed for the restriction of using hazardous substances in electrical and electronic device (EEE)”. Its objective is to restrict using six hazardous materials inside electrical and electronic system Such as Lead, Mercury, Cadmium, Hexavalent Chromium (Cr-VI), Polybrominated Biphenyl (PBB), Polybrominated Biphenyl ether (PBDE)

All applicable products in the EU market must pass the ROHS compliance after July 1, 2006. The obligatory requirement of ROHS directive is applicable for the European Union.

Benefits of ROHS Certification

  • Mandatory requirement for the European Union.
  • Improve reliability and performance of the product.
  • Improve brand value and market value within the international market
  • It helps to the business enterprise to produce secure products
  • Develops the better relationship between the patron and the enterprise
